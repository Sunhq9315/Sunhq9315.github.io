Company Introduction

Since the year 2006, Brightblue Material Technology has grown to become a leading supplier of material and components in air filtration industry in China , our main products includes aluminum foil ,metal meshes and filter plastic frames etc. Our modern facilities have a combined strength of over 120 skilled engineers, technicians, and manufacturing associates. All our facilities are ISO 9001:2015,, ISO 14001:2015 certified. At the cutting edge of technology, Brightblue has finely-honed expertise for providing solutions on designing and manufacturing complex materials and products. Welcome to “BRIGHTBLUE”, where we’re sure, you’ll discover value-added solutions to your total supply chain.

Vision

To be leading producer of highly engineered products by exceeding our customer’s expectation on Quality , Delivery ,Cost and Service.

Mission

Diversity, Equity and Inclusion

Diversity, Equity and Inclusion
The Brightblue team is striving to create and maintain a company culture that values and embraces the diversity of thought, allowing us to engage with one another and our society.

Diversity
Different perspectives ignite creativity and drive innovation. We welcome people of any race, gender, religion, ethnicity, nationality, socioeconomic status, language, ability, age, and political perspective.

Equity
We are working to establish protocols to ensure fair treatment and equal opportunity for everyone within our organization and for those who seek to join us. We’re committed to the growth needed to enact substantive reforms, and we’re committed to building a community of respect and impartiality.

Inclusion
We believe it’s essential that each person, no matter their background or perspective, feels welcome at the table and encouraged to share their ideas. If employees can’t participate fully, their unique skills go unutilized.
